Our full members

JE Global differentiates a range of member types depending on their current level of development. Our most extensive level of development is enjoyed by National Confederations – entities uniting all the national Junior Enterprises under one umbrella and by Continental Confederations, uniting the National Confederations of a whole continent.

JE Global Members

If you are interested in creating a new Junior Enterprise in a country that already has a National Confederation, you can contact them for help in getting your Junior Enterprise started. In Continental Europe, we are happy to direct you to JE Europe, our regional sister organisation.
