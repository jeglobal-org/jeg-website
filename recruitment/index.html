THE GLOBAL MISSION

An international impact adventure unlike any other

Joining JE Global, the Global Confederation of Junior Enterprises, is an extraordinary opportunity to become part of a dynamic and truly international Movement and team. With teams spanning continents, you will collaborate with talented individuals from diverse cultural and professional backgrounds. This global exposure enables you to develop a broad, international mindset, preparing you for leadership roles in a fast-changing world. Whether you are developing strategies or managing events, you will be at the forefront of a movement that champions young entrepreneurship on a global scale.

At JE Global, you can be more than just a member. By investing yourself into one of our Global Directorship roles, you can be a leader to one of 15 Global Departments. You will take charge of impactful projects that have a direct influence on shaping the future of student-led Junior Entrepreneurship. Every position offers a hands-on experience where you can enhance your leadership, strategic, and communication skills. You will work closely with the Global Executive and the Extended Executive Team in an exciting, online environment that fosters creativity and innovation. This level of responsibility and ownership ensures that you not only grow professionally but make a real difference in the global entrepreneurial landscape.

One final reason to join JE Global on the next chapter of your JE adventure is that we offer unparalleled networking opportunities. Here you can build lifelong relationships with peers and young professionals from all over the world, building or expanding your personal global network reach. Whether you are interested in strategy, governance, event planning, or communications - your contributions will be meaningful and valued. With access to international events and workshops, you’ll not only enhance your skill set but also contribute to a thriving global community of young entrepreneurs. Join us at JE Global and become part of a movement that’s changing the world!

Global Director – Strategy (m/w/d)

Location: Global, remote – 15 hrs/week

As the Director of Global Strategy, you will head the development of mid to long-term goals, growth opportunities, and operational strategies for JE Global. This role focuses on drafting and implementing Internal Policies and Standard Operating Procedures (SOPs) that ensure the organisation aligns with its mission and vision. You will lead a team of Officers, working collaboratively to deliver high-quality strategic outputs. This role offers the chance to influence the future direction of the Junior Enterprise (JE) Movement.

Note: Please consult our Recruitment Booklet for in depth requirements information or check our LinkedIn.
